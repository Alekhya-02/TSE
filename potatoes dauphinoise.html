Potatoes Dauphinoise

STEP 1:
Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5.


STEP 2:
Tip 500ml double cream, 500ml milk and 3 garlic cloves into a large
saucepan and bring to a simmer.


STEP 3:
Slice 8 large potatoes very finely, about 3-4mm, add them to the cream
and simmer for 3 mins until just cooked.


STEP 4:
Gently stir to separate the potato and stop it sinking and catching on
the bottom of the pan.


STEP 5:
increase the heat for 5 mins if not brown enough.
